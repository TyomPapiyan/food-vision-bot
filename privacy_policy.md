# Privacy Policy — FoodLens Bot

**Last updated: June 2026**

## 1. Who we are
FoodLens (@foodscannew_bot) is a Telegram bot that analyzes food photos
and provides nutritional information. Contact: @Tyom66666666

## 2. What data we collect
- **Telegram User ID** — to identify your account
- **Language preference** — to respond in your language
- **Food photos** — sent for analysis only, NOT stored on our servers
- **Food diary entries** — dish name, calories, nutrients (stored locally)
- **Subscription status** — active/inactive, expiry date

## 3. What we do NOT collect
- Your name, phone number, or email
- Your location
- Payment details (handled entirely by Telegram)
- Any data outside of this bot

## 4. How we use your data
- To provide food analysis and nutrition tracking
- To manage your subscription
- To send service notifications (subscription expiry warnings)

## 5. Data storage
All data is stored in a secure database on our server.
Food photos are sent to Google Gemini API for analysis and are
NOT stored after processing.

## 6. Third-party services
- **Google Gemini API** — for food photo analysis
  ([Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Telegram** — for payments via Telegram Stars
  ([Telegram Privacy Policy](https://telegram.org/privacy))

## 7. Data retention
Your data is stored as long as you use the bot.
To delete your data, contact @Tyom66666666.

## 8. Your rights
You can request deletion of all your data at any time
by contacting @Tyom66666666.

## 9. Changes
We may update this policy. Continued use of the bot
means you accept the updated policy.
